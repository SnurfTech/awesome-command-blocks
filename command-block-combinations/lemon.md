# How to make a lemon in Minecraft with a single command block!
Ever wanted to eat a lemon in Minecraft but couldn't use mods for whatever reason? Well you have come to the right place!

---
All you have to do is place an impulse (needs redstone, unconditional) command block with a button and this command inside:

```
/give @p minecraft:tropical_fish[minecraft:item_model=player_head,minecraft:item_name="Lemon",profile={properties:[{name:"textures",value:"eyJ0ZXh0dXJlcyI6eyJTS0lOIjp7InVybCI6Imh0dHA6Ly90ZXh0dXJlcy5taW5lY3JhZnQubmV0L3RleHR1cmUvNDM3OGI1ODJkMTljY2M1NWIwMjNlYjgyZWRhMjcxYmFjNDc0NGZhMjAwNmNmNWUxOTAyNDZlMmI0ZDVkIn19fQ=="}]}] 1
```

Then just press the button and recieve your completely edible lemon in fully vanilla Minecraft!
