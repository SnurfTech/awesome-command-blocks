# How to make a sushi in Minecraft with a single command block!
Ever wanted to eat a sushi in Minecraft but couldn't use mods for whatever reason? Well you have come to the right place!

---
All you have to do is place an impulse (needs redstone, unconditional) command block with a button and this command inside:

```
/give @p minecraft:bread[minecraft:item_model=player_head,item_name="Sushi",profile={properties:[{name:"textures",value:"eyJ0ZXh0dXJlcyI6eyJTS0lOIjp7InVybCI6Imh0dHA6Ly90ZXh0dXJlcy5taW5lY3JhZnQubmV0L3RleHR1cmUvZTUzNDdkYWRmNjgxOTlmYTdhMWI2NmYwNDgxYWQ4ZTlkYWVlMTUxMDg2NWFkZDZmMzNkMTVmYjM3OGQxM2U5MSJ9fX0="}]}] 1
```

Then just press the button and recieve your completely edible sushi in fully vanilla Minecraft!
